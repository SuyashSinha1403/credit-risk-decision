# Manual Review Playbook

## Application Completeness

For every review-band application, confirm that required identity fields,
requested amount, purpose, duration, employment status, savings indicator, and
credit-history indicator are present. If an item is absent or inconsistent,
request correction or documentation before recording a final human action.

## Employment Verification

If employment stability is a stated risk driver or cannot be supported from the
application, request recent proof of income or employment verification. The
review note should say which evidence is required and must not assume income
capacity from employment category alone.

## Loan Size And Tenure Check

For review cases involving higher amount, longer duration, or repayment burden,
review affordability evidence and confirm the requested facility is consistent
with documented ability to repay. If affordability evidence is unavailable,
place the case in a pending-document state for human handling.

## Repayment History Review

If a delayed or critical repayment-history indicator is present, request
supporting repayment evidence or bureau clarification. Escalate material
unresolved repayment concerns to a senior credit reviewer.

## Analyst Actions And Audit Trail

The analyst may request additional documents, place the application in a
callback queue, or escalate for senior review. The log should record retrieved
policy sources, documents requested, analyst identifier, timestamp, and the
human disposition with justification.

## Demo Constraint

This playbook supports retrieval-generated guidance only. A production reviewer
task queue and final decision capture are outside the current demo workflow.
